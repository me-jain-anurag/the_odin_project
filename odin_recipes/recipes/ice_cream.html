Simple fruit ice cream with minimal ingredients

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Fruit of your choice (sapota, banana etc)
  • Milk Powder
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Chop the fruits
  2. Mix and Blend everything
  3. Freeze overnight
  4. Take out and blend again
  5. Freeze 12 hrs minimum
  6. Eat
